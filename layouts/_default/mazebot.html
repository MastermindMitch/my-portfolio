What:An independent mechatronics project where a fully autonomous robot navigated an entire maze, mapped the optimal route, and completed it without ever touching any walls.

How:Used a right-mounted lidar sensor for precise wall tracking and PID control to maintain straight motion. Front and left ultrasonic sensors detected surrounding walls for mapping and corner navigation.

Results:Achieved the second fastest time out of 16 contestants.

Code:C++

Microcontroller:Arduino Mega 2560

The robot goes through each section of the maze documenting where the walls are. It never touches a wall.

Traversing the Optimal Path
